Some staffs of power holding company of Nigeria were beaten brutally

There was an incident that happenned yesterday at Itako, Ijebu ife. some PHCN official were at the community to do thier regular house to house meter inspection, every other activities were going on smoothly not until a loud voice was heard from a distance shouting for help! people rushed down to the scene to see what happened, getting there we met two of the officials lying on the ground sorrounded by thugs ready to fight.

We did some questionings and we were told there was no power supply for months and they(PHCN officials) came with enormous bills with the intention of disconnecting the wires. On no account will they do that says the community head, that argument led to the ugly scene. One of the PHCN official was badly injured but was taken to the nearby hospital for proper care.
