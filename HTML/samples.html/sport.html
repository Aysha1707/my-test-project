Villareal knocks mighty Bayern Munchen out of Ucl

Bayern Munich 1-1 Villarreal (agg 1-2): Samuel Chukwueze's late strike sends underdogs into semi-final Match report as Samuel Chukwueze's late strike sends Bayern Munich crashing out of the Champions League; Villarreal win 2-1 on aggregate after 1-1 draw at the Allianz Arena; Unai Emery's side will face Liverpool or Benfica in the semi-finals

Bayern had been pushing for a winner of their own after Robert Lewandowski cancelled out Arnaut Danjuma's first-leg strike with his 13th Champions League goal of the campaign early in the second half. But Unai Emery's side, Europa League winners against Manchester United last season, defended resolutely in the face of heavy Bayern pressure and seized their chance when it came.
