2023 Election: Nigeria Needs President Of Southeast Extraction – Group

Political parties in Nigeria, especially the People’s Democratic Party (PDP) and All Progressives Congress (APC) have been charged to choose their presidential candidates among contestants of the Southeast extraction for equity, justice and balancing purposes.

The group, PDP Coalition, in a statement by Dr. Emeka Kalu, its Coordinator, lamented that since political independence in 1960, none of the southeast origins had ever been voted as the president of the country, despite the division of Nigeria into six geo-political regions.

The group insisted that Mr. Peter Obi and Sen. Pius Anyim, the former Governor of Anambra State and former Senate President, respectively for instance from the PDP have the capacity and mental ability to transform the Nigerian economy and make it competitive with others across the globe.
